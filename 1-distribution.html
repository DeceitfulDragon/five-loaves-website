Receiving food at Five Loaves

To receive food at Five Loaves, you need to be a resident of New Richmond (WI) or live within the boundaries of the New Richmond school district.

First Visit

The first time you receive food from Five Loaves, you will need:

  • A piece of mail with your current address
  • Some form of ID with a birthdate
  • The names and age groups of those residing in your household

You must be a resident of New Richmond, Wisconsin or live within the boundaries of the New Richmond School District.

Getting Food

Clients may visit Five Loaves TWICE a month to receive food. You may come in to shop for food or receive food curbside.

Curbside List

Curbside lists are available inside the client entrance. You can also print the list below and bring it with you, or email it to:jennifer5loaves@gmail.com
