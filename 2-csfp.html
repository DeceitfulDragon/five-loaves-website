Commodity Supplemental Food program or CSFP is a monthly food program though the United States Department of Agriculture that targets senior citizens age 60 and above with an income at or below 130% of the federal poverty level.

Monthy Box Includes*:

  • 8 cans of vegetables
  • 2 shelf stable milk
  • 2 boxes of cereal
  • 3 cans of fruit
  • 1 juice
  • 3 cans of protein
  • Pasta
  • Peanut butter or nuts
  • 1 two-pound block of cheese
  • *contents subject to change
